Attack Of Titans

'Attack On Titan' is set in a post-apocalyptic world where the remains of humanity live behind walls protecting them from giant humanoid Titans. It revolves around Eren Yeager, who joins Mikasa Ackerman and Armin Arlert after a Colossal Titan breaches the wall of his hometown, destroys the city and eats his mother
